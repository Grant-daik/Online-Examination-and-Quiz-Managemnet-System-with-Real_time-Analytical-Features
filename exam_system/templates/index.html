How it works

Simple steps for students and lecturers alike.

For students
1
Register & enroll
Create your account and enroll in your courses.
2
Check your dashboard
See all active and upcoming exams for your courses.
3
Take the exam
Answer questions within the allotted time. Your progress is saved automatically.
4
View your result
See your score, grade, and full answer review immediately after submission.
For lecturers
1
Create an exam
Set the title, course, duration, and schedule for your exam.
2
Add questions
Build your question bank with Multichoice Questions, short answer, and essay types.
3
Publish & monitor
Publish the exam and watch submissions come in live.
4
Review & grade
Multichoice Questions are auto-graded. Manually grade essays and view full analytics.
