About this system

A comprehensive online examination and quiz platform built for Caritas University, designed to make academic assessments secure, efficient, and more transparent.

What is this system?

The Caritas University Online Examination & Quiz Management System is a web-based platform that allows lecturers to create and administer exams, students to take them securely from their devices, invigilators to monitor sessions in real time, and administrators to manage the entire academic structure. All this, done from one unified system.

How to use the system

🛡️
Admin
Log in and you'll be directed to the admin dashboard
Start by creating departments under Departments management.
Then create courses and assign them to departments.
Manage all users :- activate, deactivate, or delete accounts.
Assign invigilators to exams from the exam oversight page.
View system-wide analytics including submission trends and department stats.
👨‍🏫
Lecturer
Log in and you'll land on the lecturer dashboard
Click "Create exam" to set up a new exam. Give the exam a title, course, duration, and schedule.
Add questions. Choose between MCQ, short answer, or essay. For MCQ, add answer choices and mark the correct one.
Publish the exam when ready. Enrolled students will be notified automatically.
View submissions, manually grade essays, and export results to CSV or PDF.
Check analytics for grade distributions, question difficulty, and pass rates.
👨‍🎓
Student
Register an account, select the student role, and fill in your matric number, department, level, and enrolled courses.
Log in and go to your dashboard to see active and upcoming exams.
Click "Start exam" to begin. Read the instructions carefully before proceeding.
Answer all questions. Your answers are saved automatically as you proceed.
Click "Submit exam" when done or let it auto-submit when time expires.
View your result immediately, and a full review of your answers.
Track your performance trends across all exams from the My Performance page.
👀
Invigilator
You must be assigned to an exam by an admin before you can monitor it.
Log in and go to your dashboard to see your assigned exams.
Click "Monitor live" on an active exam to open the real-time monitoring dashboard.
The dashboard shows all active student sessions. Alert counts update live as suspicious events occur.
Click "Logs" on any session to see a detailed list of proctoring events for that student.
Use "Flag" to report suspicious behaviour or "Terminate" to force-submit a student's session.
